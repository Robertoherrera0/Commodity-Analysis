Price Trends

Explore how trading volumes and average prices interact. This analysis helps investors understand how volume-weighted average price(VWAP) compares with the simple moving average(SMA),offering valuable clues about underlying market momentum and potential trend shifts, uncovering key insights into market behaviour. VWMA: Reflects price trends influenced by trading volumes, highlighting the impact of transaction activity on price movements. SMA: Provides a consistent average, smoothing out price fluctuations to reveal overall market direction.

Candlestick Analysis

A green candlestick with a large body indicates strong buying pressure, suggesting a bullish market for the day. Conversely, a red candlestick with a large body points to significant selling pressure, hinting at a bearish sentiment. Small bodies with long wicks suggest indecision The mean line provides a benchmark, smoothing out daily fluctuations to show you the broader price trend
